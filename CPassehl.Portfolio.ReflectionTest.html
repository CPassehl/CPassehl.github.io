Content





Text/writing


The text is articulate and descriptive with an easy to read flow. A consistent voice is used. There are no errors in grammar, spelling, capitalization, and punctuation.


The text is basic.

There are 0 - 2 errors in grammar, spelling, capitalization, and punctuation.


The text is confusing, and has mistakes in grammar, spelling, capitalization, and punctuation.


Photographs, illustrations, artwork


Artwork is of excellent quality and supports the theme and feel of the website. Photographs are sharp and rich in tone. Artwork may be used creatively.


Artwork is of good quality. Photographs and artwork are used appropriately.


Artwork is of poor quality. Photographs may be blurry and rough in contrast/value.

Design





Creativity and design


Site has a creative and thoughtful tone. Excellent integration of text to theme to art to functionality.


Good sense of design and tone. Artwork in general syncs with topics and categories and supports the narrative.


Design looks clumsy and unsophisticated. Artwork does not support text and categories.


Layout


Website is a pleasure to visit. Layout is exceptionally designed with elements logically placed.


Layout is good and functional, but not terribly exciting. Important elements can be found with little to no trouble.


Website is rough and not user- friendly. Elements are hard to find. Styles are inconsistent and make things appear busy.


Theme consistency


Theme utilizes a consistent use of colors, fonts and treatment of graphics. Theme supports high degree of clarity in purpose and vision.


Theme utilizes a consistent use of colors, fonts and treatment of graphics.


Theme feels disjointed with elements incorporating different styles, colors and fonts.

Technical





Navigation


LInk categories and topics are easy to read and understand. Easy to transition from page to page.


LInk categories and topics are correctly labeled and function.


Links are rough and may not function correctly or have awkward transitions from page to page.


Responsive


Site functions flawlessly regardless of device.


Site functions well regardless of device.


Site is not responsive to changes in device and screen size.


Performance


Site loads quickly with animations and javascript working correctly.


Site loads smoothly, but not in the fastest fashion.


Site takes too long to load and/or javascript does not load
