Many artists have been influenced by Josef Albers. I am in that class.
My code drawing is a simple homage to the masterful patterns and compositions of Albers. Along with the distinct and simple arrangements seen in "Prefatio", there exists a confidence and clarity in his composition.
The code sketch shown alludes to the patterns of Albers by the variation of the textures created by the spacing of the circles and cubes. Concluding the sketch is the viewers choice as determined by how many mouse clicks are entered.
